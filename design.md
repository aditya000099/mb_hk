# Modern Reddit UI Design Specifications

## Color Palette
- **Background**: `#000000` (Pure Black)
- **Surface**: `#0f1113` (Very dark gray for cards/containers)
- **Raised Surface**: `#1A282D` or `#272729` (Hover states and pill buttons)
- **Brand Primary**: `#FF4500` (Reddit Orange)
- **Brand Hover**: `#e03d00`
- **Text Primary**: `#FFFFFF` or `#d7dadc`
- **Text Muted**: `#82959b` or `#818384`

## Post Card UI
- **Container**: No outer border, or very subtle border. Background `#0f1113`. Padding `16px`.
- **Header**: 
  - Subreddit/Author Avatar: Small circular image (24x24).
  - Subreddit Name / Author Name: Bold text.
  - Timestamp: Muted text alongside the author.
  - Three dots (options) icon on the top right.
- **Title**: Large (e.g. 18px-20px), bold, white/light-gray text.
- **Flair**: Pill-shaped, tight padding, vivid background (e.g. Yellow) with contrasting text (e.g. Black).
- **Body**: Medium text size (14px), gray-ish. Links are typically primary color or standard link color (blue-ish) with underline on hover.
- **Action Bar (Bottom)**:
  - Consists of pill-shaped buttons with a background color of `#272729`.
  - Vote Pill: Up arrow icon, vote count, Down arrow icon.
  - Comment Pill: Chat icon, comment count.
  - Award Pill: Ribbon icon.
  - Share Pill: Curved arrow icon, "Share" text.

## Sidebar
- Left Sidebar with sticky navigation.
- Top Subreddits / Popular Communities on the Right Sidebar for home feeds.
- Sleek hover effects on list items.
